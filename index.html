Unit 5 Final Design Project

The Museum of You

"Every person is a walking civilization. It is time to curate your legacy."

The Commission

The Global Heritage Foundation has granted you unlimited funds to build a brand-new museum. However, this museum cannot hold ancient mummies or dinosaur bones. It must be dedicated entirely to your life, your memories, and your favorite things.

The Architecture

Every great museum needs an identity. Decide on the following for your museum:

  • The Name: (e.g., The Gallery of Emre, The Museum of My Teenage Years)
  • The Building: Is it a glass pyramid? An underwater dome? A cozy wooden cabin?
  • Vocabulary Check: Write a 2-sentence mission statement using at least two words from the unit (e.g., preserve, legacy, heritage, civilization, exhibit).
2

The Collection

Select three specific items from your life that you absolutely love. These are your "Artifacts." They could be:

Your favorite hoodie A concert ticket A guitar pick Your skateboard

For each item, you must find a photo (or draw it) to display in your final presentation.

3

The Speculative Plaques

Museum plaques explain what objects are. Write a label for each of your 3 artifacts. You must include one sentence of "future speculation" for each.

Example Plaque:

The Fender Stratocaster Guitar

"This object produced loud, echoing noises. Visitors from the year 3000 might think it was a weapon used to scare enemies. It must have required great skill to hold, but it can't have been comfortable to sleep with."

Final Deliverable

Create a PowerPoint, Canva Presentation, or a physical poster board showcasing your museum's architecture and your 3 artifacts with their plaques. Prepare a 2-minute "Audio Tour" to present to the class!
