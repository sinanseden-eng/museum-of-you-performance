Unit 5 Final Design Project

The Museum of You

"Every person is a walking civilization. It is time to curate your legacy."

The Commission

The Global Heritage Foundation is funding a brand-new museum dedicated entirely to your life, memories, and values.

Your task is to design a visually stunning, persuasive Visitor Brochure to attract tourists and explain the critical vision behind your collection.

The Front Cover: Vision

To attract visitors, your brochure needs a powerful cover. Decide on:

  • The Name: (e.g., The Gallery of Emre, The Teenage Archives)
  • The Hook: A catchy slogan to make tourists want to enter.
  • The Vision Statement: Write 2 sentences using unit vocabulary (e.g., preserve, legacy, heritage, exhibit) explaining *why* this museum was built.
2

Inside Pages: Exhibit Highlights

Select three specific items from your life (e.g., a skateboard, a concert ticket, a favorite hoodie). Include a photo for each, accompanied by a Speculative Description to entertain your visitors.

‚ö†Ô∏è Grammar Requirement: You must guess how historians in the year 3000 will interpret each item using modals (must have, might be, can't have).

Example Brochure Text:

Exhibit 1: The Stratocaster Guitar

"This object produced loud, echoing noises. Future historians might think it was a weapon used to scare enemies. It must have required great skill to hold, but it can't have been comfortable to sleep with."

3

Back Cover: Curator's Philosophy

This is where you show deep critical thinking. Visitors want to know the meaning behind the collection. Write a final paragraph for the back cover answering this question:

"If this museum burns down in a thousand years, and archaeologists can only save one of your artifacts, which one should it be? What does that single item teach the future about what the youth of the 21st century truly valued?"

Final Deliverable

Use a template tool (like Canva's "Tri-Fold Brochure" or Google Docs) to design your final product. Pay attention to layout, images, and visual appeal. Submit your digital brochure PDF to the class folder!
